My website - 22/03/2021

I took the challange to study HTML,PHP, CSS ,JavaScript and build my website from scratch (Learning the Web Development tools was a piece of cake 70% of my website was done in day 1 of development the other 30% was developing the API and framework to import various data from my other social media sources). I purchased a domain from GoDaddy - ADGSTUDIOS.co.za is my first ever domain i have purchased. In the past i did make websites with WordPress and Weebly.com. I am at home taking a Gap Year. This website is just one of my projects used to sharpen my skills and improve my knowlege. I know 10 languages in computers now i can successfully say i can add HTML,PHP,CSS,JavaScript to my experience. When i am free i am still going to experiment on this site. I am planning to put my Web Unity Games and implement a MySQL database on this website. My site is my place to experiement with code and ideas this is my playground. The best way to learn programming and coding is to do projects and use Google as a guide to help you when you are stuck. A programmers job is basically googling and modifying code to your liking.

This website is powered by a Raspberry Pi 4 via Apache Server - 4GB RAM 64bit (Ubuntu Server) It is quite inexpensive hardware, My Pi 4 costed me R1000. Paying for webhosting will cost you around R300 pm for your website. I am just paying R100 for my domain per annum.
